Welcome to GeneDrNetwork

GeneDrNetwork is a genetics-focused platform dedicated to connecting clinical care, education, and community programs for patients, families, clinicians, and learners.

Programs

Program Clinical Genetics Network (CGN)

Clinical genetics expertise, referral pathways, education, and coordinated care.

 Program Rare Disease Day

Awareness, education, and collaboration for rare disease care and diagnosis.

 Program Genetics Education

Practical genetics learning for clinicians, trainees, and community partners.

 Program Therapeutics & Technology Updates

Academic updates on emerging therapies and genetic testing technologies.

 Program Research

Clinical research connections, registries, natural history studies, and collaborations.

Mission

Advancing genetics and improving lives through compassionate care, practical education, rare disease awareness, and collaborative clinical networks.
