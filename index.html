About GeneDrNetwork

GeneDrNetwork is a professional platform designed to advance clinical genetics through education, collaboration, and improved access to genetics expertise.

Programs

Clinical Genetics Network

A collaborative network supporting clinical genetics, rare disease diagnosis, and multidisciplinary care.

Genetics Education

Educational resources, lectures, and training materials for clinicians, trainees, and healthcare teams.

Rare Disease Day

Awareness, advocacy, and education for rare diseases and genomic medicine.

Future Centers

Metabolic genetics, skeletal dysplasia, mitochondrial disease, cancer predisposition, and precision medicine.

Mission

Our mission is to build a sustainable clinical genetics network that promotes education, collaboration, early diagnosis, and better care for individuals and families affected by genetic disorders.
