Program Overview

Genetics Education supports clinicians, trainees, learners, and community partners through academic and practical teaching in genetics and genomics.

Educational Topics

  • Genetic testing selection and diagnostic strategy
  • Variant interpretation and clinical reporting concepts
  • Genetic counseling principles and family communication
  • Rare disease, obesity medicine, and genomics-informed care
