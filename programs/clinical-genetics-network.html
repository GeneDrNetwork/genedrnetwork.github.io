Program Overview

Clinical Genetics Network (CGN)/Rare Disease Day connects clinical genetics expertise, referral pathways, diagnostic evaluation, family-centered care, rare disease awareness, and education for patients with genetic or suspected genetic conditions.

Academic Focus

  • Clinical genetics consultation and care coordination concepts
  • Rare disease awareness, education, and community engagement
  • Referral pathway development and interdisciplinary communication
  • Genetic testing education, interpretation support, and diagnostic collaboration
