Program Overview

The Clinical Genetics Network (CGN) is focused on connecting clinical genetics expertise, referral pathways, diagnostic evaluation, family-centered care, and education for patients with genetic or suspected genetic conditions.

Academic Focus

  • Clinical genetics consultation and care coordination concepts
  • Referral pathway development and interdisciplinary communication
  • Genetic testing education and interpretation support
  • Rare and undiagnosed disease clinical collaboration
