Program Overview

Therapeutics & Technology Updates is a GeneDrNetwork program for professional review of emerging clinical therapeutics, genomic technologies, and diagnostic platforms relevant to genetics and rare disease care.

Content is intended for academic and clinical education and is non-commercial.

Therapeutics Updates

Updates may include new treatments, gene therapy, RNA therapy, enzyme replacement therapy, targeted therapy, and clinical trial updates.

  • New treatments and evolving standards of care
  • Gene therapy and genome-targeted therapeutic approaches
  • RNA therapy, including antisense and RNA-based treatment strategies
  • Enzyme replacement therapy and metabolic disease treatment updates
  • Targeted therapy and precision medicine developments
  • Clinical trial updates relevant to genetic and rare disease care

Technology Updates

Updates may include WGS, long-read sequencing, RNA sequencing, methylation testing, optical genome mapping, AI-assisted variant interpretation, and new genetic testing platforms.

  • Whole genome sequencing (WGS)
  • Long-read sequencing
  • RNA sequencing
  • Methylation testing
  • Optical genome mapping
  • AI-assisted variant interpretation
  • New genetic testing platforms and diagnostic workflows
