Program Overview

Rare Disease Day is a GeneDrNetwork program for clinical awareness, education, family support, interdisciplinary collaboration, and research connections related to rare and undiagnosed diseases.

Program Scope

  • Rare disease diagnosis and clinical care education
  • Genomics and precision medicine discussions
  • Patient, family, clinician, and trainee engagement
  • Regional collaboration and resource development
