IEM Content Preserved

The Inborn Error of Metabolism (IEM)/Metabolic Disorder Quick Guide is a GeneDrNetwork resource for practical education around metabolic red flags, initial evaluation, timely referral, and coordinated care for patients with suspected inherited metabolic disorders. This content is now part of Genetics Education & Professional Guidelines.

Guide Scope

  • Clinical red flags for inborn errors of metabolism and metabolic disorders
  • Initial laboratory evaluation and urgent triage concepts
  • Referral pathways for genetics, metabolic, and specialty care
  • Education for clinicians, trainees, families, and community partners
