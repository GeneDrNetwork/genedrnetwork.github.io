Program Overview

The Inborn Error of Metabolism (IEM)/Metabolic Disorder Quick Guide is a GeneDrNetwork program for practical education around metabolic red flags, initial evaluation, timely referral, and coordinated care for patients with suspected inherited metabolic disorders.

Guide Scope

  • Clinical red flags for inborn errors of metabolism and metabolic disorders
  • Initial laboratory evaluation and urgent triage concepts
  • Referral pathways for genetics, metabolic, and specialty care
  • Education for clinicians, trainees, families, and community partners
